Combining Promises

Group multiple promises to produce a single promise with the ".all()" method. 

$q.all([promiseA, promiseB]).then(function(promisesSuccessResult){
	console.log(promisesSuccessResult[0]) //promiseA result
	console.log(promisesSuccessResult[1]) //promiseB result
});

Note: jQuery's '$.when()' method does the same thing.
